About For All

Our Mission

For All is a community resource platform designed to connect Baltimore residents with essential services including housing assistance, food pantries, and clothing resources. We believe everyone deserves access to the support they need, and we're here to make finding those resources as simple as possible.

What We Do

Our platform helps you:

  • Find housing resources, emergency shelters, and transitional housing programs
  • Locate food pantries, meal kitchens, and emergency food assistance
  • Access clothing closets and professional attire services
  • Get personalized recommendations based on your specific needs

Our Partners

We work with trusted community organizations across Baltimore, including:

  • Weinberg Housing & Resource Center
  • Sarah's Hope Shelter
  • Project PLASE
  • Helping Up Mission
  • Bea Gaddy Family Center
  • Beans and Bread
  • Paul's Place Clothing Marketplace
  • And many more community partners

How to Use For All

Get Started: Describe what you need on our home page, and we'll recommend the best resource for you.

Browse by Category: Explore housing, food, or clothing resources by category.

Search: Use our search feature to find specific services or organizations.

Emergency Help: If you need immediate assistance, call 211 for 24/7 support.

Contact & Support

For questions or to add your organization to our directory, please reach out through our NGO Portal or contact your local community resource center.

Remember: In case of emergency, always call 211 or 911.
